# HR Policy Manual — TechCorp India

## Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid leave per year.
Sick leave is capped at 10 days annually with a medical certificate required after 3 consecutive days.
Casual leave: 8 days per year, cannot be carried forward.

## Refund & Expense Policy
Business travel expenses must be submitted within 7 days of the trip.
Hotel reimbursement limit: ₹5,000/night for domestic travel.
Flight bookings must be economy class unless approved by VP.

## Performance Reviews
Reviews happen twice a year: June and December.
Employees scoring below 3/5 for two consecutive cycles are placed on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).
Bonus eligibility requires a minimum rating of 3.5/5.

## Onboarding
New hires receive a 2-week orientation program.
Laptop and equipment are provided on Day 1.
System access is configured within 24 hours of joining.
