# Payslip Policy Manual — TechCorp India

## Payslip Generation

Payslips are generated on the last working day of each month.
Employees can access their payslips through the Employee Self-Service (ESS) portal.
Payslips remain available for download for up to 7 years.

## Salary Components

Payslips include Basic Pay, House Rent Allowance (HRA), Special Allowance, and other applicable earnings.
Deductions such as Provident Fund (PF), Professional Tax (PT), Income Tax (TDS), and other statutory deductions are clearly itemized.
Net Pay is calculated after all deductions.

## Payslip Distribution

Payslips are provided in PDF format.
Employees receive an email notification once the payslip is available.
Physical copies are not issued unless required for official purposes.

## Payslip Corrections

Any discrepancy in salary or deductions must be reported to the Payroll Team within 15 days of payslip issuance.
Correction requests should include supporting documents where applicable.
Approved corrections will be reflected in the subsequent payroll cycle or through an off-cycle adjustment.

## Confidentiality

Payslips contain confidential employee information and should not be shared with unauthorized individuals.
Employees are responsible for safeguarding downloaded payslip copies.
Unauthorized disclosure of payroll information may result in disciplinary action.

## Tax and Compliance

Annual tax declarations must be submitted before the deadline communicated by HR.
Form 16 is issued annually for income tax filing purposes.
Statutory deductions are processed in accordance with applicable Indian labor and tax laws.

## Employment Verification

Payslips may be used as proof of employment and income.
Employees requiring salary certificates for loans, visas, or other official purposes may request them through HR.
Processing time for salary certificate requests is typically 3 business days.
